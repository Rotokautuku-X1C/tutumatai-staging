Our People

The descendants of Raana Te Iti Walker and Mere Roa Milner — the whānau at the heart of Tutumatai Station.

Ngā uri — First Generation

The First Generation

The first generation — the tamariki of Raana Te Iti Walker and Mere Roa Milner, shown with their spouses. Names, spellings and nicknames to be confirmed by the COM; photographs to be added.

Raana Tuterangi (Boy)
1909 – 1985
m. Ani Reedy

Following the unexpected death of Papa Raana Te Iti in 1932, Boy took over running the farm with Nanny Walker, at the age of 22 — with 13 children at home aged from 3 to 22.
