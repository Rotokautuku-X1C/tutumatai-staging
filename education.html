Eligibility & criteria

Who can apply

  • All descendants of Raana Te Iti Walker and Mere Roa Milner are eligible to apply
  • Must be enrolled in tertiary study at a University, Polytechnic, Wānanga, or approved private training establishment
  • The course must lead to a qualification or further opportunity — including trades and apprenticeships
  • Evidence of enrolment must be provided
  • Repeat applicants must provide evidence of achievement from the previous year
  • Applicants developing farming and farm management skills are particularly encouraged
  • Grants are awarded at the discretion of the Committee of Management subject to available funds
